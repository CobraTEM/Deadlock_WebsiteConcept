FATHOM
Slips into combat and then hides to recover
A short-ranged assassin, Fathom can slip in and out of combat and win long engagements.
Scalding Spray
Spray scalding water in a cone ahead of you. You build up bonus weapon damage as the spray damages an enemy hero.
Breach
Launch forward, damaging all enemies near where you land and knocking them into the air.
Reefdweller Harpoon
Throw a harpoon that latches to a wall or ceiling, and pulls you in. Activates Lurker's Ambush's invisibility while being pulled in. Once hanging attached to the harpoon surface, gain additional charges.
Lurker's Ambush
Hold 4 to charge up a mouthful of sticky spit that damages and slows enemies. Damage and slow duration increase the longer you hold the spit. Duration is halved if you are not latched to a wall.
