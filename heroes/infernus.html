INFERNUS
Lights up enemies and watches them burn
Infernus has many ways to deal damage over time, burning foes before swooping in for the kill. Due to Infernus' blazing speed, his enemies won't be able to escape the flames.
Catalyst
Spew an incendiary mixture, dealing spirit damage, applying slow, and coating targets in napalm.
Flame Dash
Dash forward, gaining slow resistance while leaving a flaming trail that deals spirit damage over time. Hold Forward while active to dash farther.
Afterburn
Weapon hits build up a burning effect, dealing spirit damage over time. Bullets and abilities refresh the burn duration.
Concussive Combustion
Become a living bomb, dealing spirit damage and applying stun to all nearby enemies after a delay.
