TRAPPER
Entrap enemies and control space
The Trapper can infest the battlefield with their haunted spiders, leaving latent curses in key areas. If you let the Trapper have their way, you won't be able to navigate the map without springing some sort of trap.
Bottled Phantasmicide
Throw a jar that leaves a spreading toxic pool. Enemies caught in the pool are Slowed and Silenced.
Silktrap
Create a thread of web between two surfaces. Enemies who touch the web take Damage over time, become Slowed and are Revealed.
Pest Barrier
Apply a Combat Barrier to a target ally and periodically deal Spirit Damage and Slows.
Crawling Plague
Release a wave of spectral spiders that chase enemies and explode, dealing Spirit Damage. Hit enemies are poisoned, temporarily losing Spirit and Spirit Resist. This effect can stack multiple times.
