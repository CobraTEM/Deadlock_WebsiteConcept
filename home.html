Deadlock is a 6v6 team-based third-person shooter and MOBA. In each match, the 12 players control unique heroes who begin split across three lanes in a large map. To win, one team has to push their troopers down the lanes and destroy the opposing team's patron, a large supernatural entity located in the team's stronghold.
