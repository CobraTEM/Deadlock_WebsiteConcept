What is Deadlock?

Deadlock is a 6v6 team-based third-person shooter and MOBA game developed and published by Valve. In each match, 12 players control unique "heroes" who begin split across three lanes in a large map. To win, one team has to push their troopers down the lanes and destroy the opposing team's patron, a large supernatural entity located in the team's stronghold. Since April 2024, Deadlock has been in early development playtesting.
